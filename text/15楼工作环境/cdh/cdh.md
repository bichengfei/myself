## 启动

1. 登录cdh1
2. 执行脚本cdh.sh  
   `./cdh.sh start`
3. 登录CDH web管理工具  
   浏览器输入： `cdh1:7180`  
   user: `admin`  
   password:`admin`
4. 启动 Cloudera Management Service  
   ![Cloudera Management Service](./CMS.JPG)
5. 启动Cluster  
   ![Cluster](./Cluster.JPG)

## 关闭

1. 登录CDH web管理工具  
   浏览器输入： `cdh1:7180`  
   user: `admin`  
   password:`admin`
2. 停止 Cloudera Management Service  
   ![Cloudera Management Service](./CMS.JPG)
3. 停止 Cluster  
   ![Cluster](./Cluster.JPG)
4. 登录cdh1
5. 执行脚本cdh.sh  
   `./cdh.sh stop`
